Disclaimers

The liability of On-Time Couriers, Inc. and its contractors (collectively “OTC”) is limited to $100.00 for lost, delayed, or damaged items. If, at the time of booking the delivery, the customer declares, to OTC, that the items tendered to OTC for that delivery have a higher replacement value than $100, then OTC's liability shall, for that delivery, be increased to the declared replacement value or $1,000.00, whichever is less. The customer may, for any delivery and through a written agreement with additional payment, arrange for liability greater than $1,000.00. Any claim for loss, delay, or damage, must be reported to office@ontimecouriers.com within 24 hours of the actual delivery time.

Family-Owned Since 1951

The Gumm Family began the first established local delivery service in Dallas/Ft. Worth. In 1951, while working as a local cab driver, William E. Gumm was dispatched to pick up flowers from a florist and deliver them to Baylor Hospital. He immediately saw an opportunity to capitalize on an untapped market ... fast, reliable, and courteous delivery service to the Dallas area. Mr. Gumm's business originated out of his garage in Oak Cliff, and eventually moved to a downtown office in 1954.

By the late 1980's, his personable grandson, Barry J. Gumm took the knowledge that his grandfather had taught him and began what is now known as the fastest courier service in Dallas, On Time Couriers.

Our Success Continues Today

Four generations later, the courier business continues on. On Time is currently owned and operated by William E. Gumm's great-granddaughter and Barry J. Gumm's daughter, Megan Gumm, along with several other extended members of the Gumm family. Our mission remains the same...The Customer is King.
